Map Editor Tutorial:

Place Decimator
Activate
Destroy all 4 Corners of map
Press 8 to Place map
Press 9 to Increase Size of Future Spawned Maps
Press 0 To Decrease Size of Future Spawned Maps

Unfortunately at this time there is no way to undo
you will either have to restart, or use the decimator to destroy the changes
